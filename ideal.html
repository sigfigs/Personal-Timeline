Ideal Future: Career

My ideal future starts with going to a top university. From there, I want to pursue my passion for computer science. Hopefully, after four years, the skills built up will help me land a career at a big tech company like Facebook, Amazon, Apple, Netflix, and Google. Relating this to Death of a Salesman by Arthur Miller, I agree with Willy Loman’s mindset of success – that money will bring happiness. The most important factor that influenced my beliefs would have to be my family’s background and childhood. I want a future that they never had.
