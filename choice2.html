Choice 2: Teaching

I chose to start teaching others.

Throughout my life, I have always been a student. However, my position switched to the other side in high school when I got my first volunteering job. As the pandemic wiped out in-person learning, many young children were lost. Not all schools could provide high-quality remote learning replacement, and not all children were ready to meet new challenges with fewer classes and interactions with their peers. I joined “Remote Buddies” by mentoring disadvantaged elementary and middle school students. It gave me the opportunity to teach, from which I developed a new perspective about learning. Being able to reach out and help those in need made me proud. With this teaching experience, I was successfully hired as a paid tutor.

My experiences in teaching showed me that trying something new will benefit others but also yourself in the process. High school may seem never-ending, but this experience gave a purpose to attending classes and studying.
