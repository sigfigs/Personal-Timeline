Support System: Parents

The biggest support system I have are my parents. However, during the middle of 8th grade, my father suddenly disappeared. Although I have seen this happen in movies and books many times, it was shocking that I would experience it myself. Despite the fact that he had never done much for my family, I was nevertheless saddened because I would not have a “normal” life, as one of the supporting pillars in my life crashed overnight. However, I did not let this one instance stop me from reaching my goals. Instead, it pushed me harder to become more resourceful and made me more resilient and independent.
