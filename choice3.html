Choice 3: Idolizing

I chose who I idolize.

As social creatures, humans depend on guidance from others. Ever since I was young, I knew that surrounding myself with people I know will benefit me and support me through my obstacles.

At school, I idolize the intelligent, hardworking students. The ones who always know what to say when a teacher calls on them. The ones who have good study habits and receive straight As. Although some say comparing yourself to others is unhealthy, I believe it keeps me in check, whether my pacing and workload is too little or too much. When I finally make friends with them, we share our experiences that led to success.

At home, I idolize my mom, a strong, persevering woman who supported me my entire life. She’s shaped and taught me more than any person through her actions. Her circumstances weren’t as fortunate, which motivates me to improve my life in the future.
